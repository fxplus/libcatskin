The title list summarizes your search results.

To display the record details associated with a title, click on the underlined title to jump to the details. You can page through your results by using the navigation bar.

If you want to save or print information about specific records,

  1. Mark the record(s) you are interested in by clicking the checkbox on the left side of the screen.
  2. Click an option button at the top or buttom of the list.
  3. Follow the instructions on the page that follows.

Clicking the Select Page or All checkbox will allow you to select all results on the page or from the search for printing or exporting.
