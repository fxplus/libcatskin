Library Catalogue Change Pin Help

A Personal Identification Number (PIN) is a security feature. You are required to provide your PIN whenever you log in. PINs are alphanumeric and between 5 and 12 characters long. They are also case sensitive. (Special characters and symbols should not be used in your PIN.)

If you are logging in for the first time, you must enter the default PIN established by the library and you must create a new PIN.

Changing your PIN

  1. Enter your current PIN or library default PIN.
  2. Enter your new PIN twice. It will be masked for your protection and must be identical.
  3. Press the Save button.
